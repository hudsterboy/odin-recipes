Carolina Red Rice and Chicken

This is a terrific recipe for Carolina Red Rice and Chicken. You will LIKE or DIE

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 3.5 cups water
  • 1 chicken bone broth ice cube
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 diced red pepper
    • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 1 breast of chicken diced
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • Olive Oil
  • 1/2 can tomato paste

Steps

  1. In a 5 quart sauce pan, fry onions, peppers and garlic in olive oil
  2. Add chicken, and fry until just starting to brown
  3. Add chicken stock and 2 3/4 cup water
  4. Add tomato paste
  5. Bring to boil, then add rice
  6. Bring to simmer
  7. Simmer for around 30-40 min or until water absorbed by rice
  8. Remove from heat and let sit for 5 min
  9. Mix and serve
