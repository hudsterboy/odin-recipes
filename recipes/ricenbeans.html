Rice and Beans

This is a tasty recipe for rice and beans. Eat it, bitch.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 3.5 cups water
  • 1 chicken bone broth ice cube
  • 1 onion
    • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 large can pinto beans
  • 1 cup chicken boullion
  • 1 packets Sazon
  • Chili Powder
  • Olive Oil
  • salsa

Steps

  1. In a sauce pan, add water and chicken boullion
  2. Bring to a boil, then add rice. Cover and simmer
  3. In a cast iron fyring pan, heat olive oil. Add chopped onions and minced garlic
  4. Cook until translucent
  5. Drain canned beans and add to onions
  6. Cover beans with water and add about 1/2 cup of Salsa
  7. Add 2 fl ounce chicken bone broth
  8. Add packet of Sazon
  9. add about 1 tsp chili powder
  10. stir the Ingredients, smashing some of the beans
  11. simmer beans for around 30 min, adding water as needed to maintain desired consistency
  12. Service beans with rice
